About

A firm built on careful work.

Oxford Advocates was formed by Ugandan lawyers who wanted to practise differently — fewer files, deeper preparation, and the kind of plain talk clients actually need.

Home / About
Our story

From a single chambers in Mengo.

We opened our doors opposite Sir Apollo Kaggwa Road with a small library, a long table, and a list of clients we already knew. The work taught us the rest.

Today we counsel Ugandan businesses, families and individuals across the country — but we still keep our case load deliberately small. A lawyer who is everywhere is rarely anywhere, and our clients deserve someone who is actually present.

We are proud to call Mengo home. The neighbourhood reminds us, every morning, that law begins where people live.

Values that show up in the work.

Excellence
